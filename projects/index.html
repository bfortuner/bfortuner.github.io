  • The Yellow Journal  2011

    Helped relaunch University of Virgina's oldest humor publication, Ye Yellow Journal. Founded in 1912 under the slogan "All The News That Is Unfit To Print," the Journal was discontinued in 1934 when the University's Administrative Council set forth an order forbidding "the publication or sale of any anonymous paper", citing material they found "scurrilous and indecent in the extreme." Today the Journal is published semesterly and features satirical news articles centered around student life at UVA.
